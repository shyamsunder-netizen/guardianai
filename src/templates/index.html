About GuardianAI

GuardianAI is an advanced cybersecurity research platform engineered to detect phishing and malicious URLs using a hybrid multi-layer intelligence architecture.

The system integrates machine learning models, entropy-based anomaly detection, domain structure analysis, brand impersonation similarity scoring, and infrastructure risk evaluation to produce accurate real-time threat assessment.

Unlike traditional rule-based scanners, GuardianAI applies behavioral intelligence and adaptive threat scoring to identify sophisticated phishing campaigns, typo-squatting attacks, randomly generated domains, and malicious hosting abuse.

Machine Learning Core

Random Forest classifier trained on large-scale phishing and legitimate datasets with ROC-AUC validation and cross-validation testing.

Entropy & Anomaly Detection

Detects high-randomness domains, encoded patterns, and algorithmically generated phishing URLs.

Brand Impersonation Analysis

Levenshtein distance-based similarity detection identifies domain spoofing and typo-squatting attacks targeting trusted brands.

Hybrid Threat Scoring Engine

Multi-factor adaptive scoring system combining ML confidence with behavioral signals for comprehensive risk classification.
